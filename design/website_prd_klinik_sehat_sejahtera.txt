# Klinik Sehat Sejahtera - Website Plan

## Target Audience
Patients looking for reliable healthcare services, mothers (KIA), and people needing medical check-ups.

## Sitemap
1. **Homepage**: Hero section, About, Key Advantages, Service Overview, Testimonials, Insurance Partners.
2. **Services & Facilities**: Detailed cards for General Check-ups, KIA, Lab, Khitan, MCU, and a Facility Gallery.
3. **Our Team**: Doctor profiles with specialties and schedules.
4. **Booking & Schedule**: Online registration form and real-time doctor availability calendar.
5. **Health Blog & FAQ**: Educational articles and frequently asked questions.

## Key Features
- Floating WhatsApp button for fast response.
- Integrated Google Maps for location.
- Multi-language support (Indonesian as primary).
- Mobile-responsive design (Desktop target first).
